Cake

The cake is a lie.

Ingredients

  • 3 bundles of wheat
  • 1 egg
  • 3 buckets of milk
  • 2 pieces of sugar

Directions

  1. Open the crafting table
  2. Place the three buckets of milk straight across the top, first row
  3. At the very middle, place the egg
  4. In the second row, place the pieces of sugar to the left and right of the egg
  5. Place the three bundles of wheat straight across the bottom, last row
